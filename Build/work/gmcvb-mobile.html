Client
Greater Miami Convention & Visitors Bureau

Continuing the unique brand story of Miami, a mobile site was created for on-the-ground visitors.

When tourists arrive in Miami, the site allows visitors to continue the web platform as their source for inspiration, discovery, and recommendations. The team aimed to deliver all of this content for quick and easy use at a visitor’s fingertips.

Using geo-location, the team was able to suggest curated unique attractions within a short distance. The mobile site brought the Miami experience full circle from conception to fruition.

Mobile Homepage

The goal of the Miami mobile homepage was to quickly answer the question of “What shall we do next?” with memorable attractions and sightseeing.
