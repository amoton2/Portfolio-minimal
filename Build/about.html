About me

I am Antonio Moton II, an Art Director and Designer living in Brooklyn, NY.

01

My fundamental training is in traditional layouts, typography, and brand identity. Since then, I've grown a passion for user experience and web design. Over the past several years, I've had the opportunity to work on some interesting brands, such as Greater Miami Convention & Visitors Bureau, Infinti, United Airlines, Illinois Lottery, United Services Automobile Association (USAA), Humana, Nationwide Insurance, Zurich Financial Services Group, Cleveland Clinic, and many other local companies. I approach every opportunity with a fresh eye, determination, and pride for my craft.

As an employee, I am direct, flexible, and proactive toward the client's needs and your needs as an employer. When I'm not creating something cool, I like to keep things smooth and simple. I spend my free time driving my lovely wife crazy, playing with my killer of a dog, Rico, enjoying life, and traveling.
